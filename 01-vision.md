# Vision

## Mission

Help businesses discover commercial opportunities before they become obvious.

---

## The Problem

Companies continuously generate public business signals.

Unfortunately those signals are scattered across hundreds of different sources.

By the time a salesperson notices them, the opportunity has often disappeared.

Current tools focus on data.

TIG focuses on intent.

---

## The Solution

TIG continuously collects public business evidence.

The platform converts evidence into signals.

Signals are analysed by industry-specific scoring models.

The result is a ranked list of companies that are likely to require products or services.

Rather than asking:

"What happened?"

TIG answers:

"What should I do next?"
